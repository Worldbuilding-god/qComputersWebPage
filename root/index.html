Quantum Computers: The next step‽

Quantum computers are a relatively new technology. They blend the mathematics of computing with the sciences of the quantum world. Most of the time they are harder to build.

Quantum computers can be understood as a special type of computing machine. Quantum computers use a type of computing base called "Qubits" which can be both "On' or "Off" simultaneously. This allows better, faster more complex, and stronger calculations to be preformed. This is due to some complex mathematics like the Bloch sphere, or complex numbers. Complex numbers are like normal numbers. Imagine an X Y plane, but instead of an X and Y axis, there is the normal number line, and the complex number line. This is the complex plane. One can shift into the complex by making the square root of a negative number, like √-1.
